About

I am an entrepreneur, a franlancer or just a free spirit who like solving problems. In fact, what I would like to be called is: a problem solver. I am from Rennes (Brittany, France) and after pursuing a hospitality diploma and a Hospitality Bachelor degree in Switzerland along with many international experiences, I decided to open my own restaurant. It seems to be a logical outcome given my background, yet, not logical enough for me to be fulfilled. Back in 2014, I got introduced to web design and web programming, it subsequently changed my life plans.

I decided to go through the painfull process of self-teaching myself the skills I wanted to have, combined with online schools programs. At that time, I had to manage my very busy restaurant, do programming and coding in my free time (usally till very late at night) and still be a good husband. After working on many different web projects, I decided to sell my restaurant, setlle down with my wife in Mexico (she is mexican) and to fully dedicate myself to the Web industry by creating Monolok.

Monolok's mission is to simplify businesses daily operations and their web needs. I offer web-app based solutions to make business owner's life easier. I know what it means to run a business and the diffrent problems to adress to (HR, operations and marketing among others). My wish is to make businesses daily operations and their web needs cleaner by respecting and enhancing the work of my clients. Four very simple premises drive me: honoring deadlines; doing the extra mile; giving an honest and friendly customer-oriented service and being highly flexible.
